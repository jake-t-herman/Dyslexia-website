Who am I?

Hello! my name is Sarah and I live in East Sussex with my husband, two children, Nova the cat, and Pip the dog!

What are my qualifications?

I have a BA (Hons) in Modern History, a PGCE in primary education and am currently completing a level 5 certificate in Dyslexia; Literacy, Support, and Intervention. I was a primary school teacher for 14 years. I have an enhanced DBS check, QTS (Qualified Teacher Status) and am up to date with relevant safeguarding training.
